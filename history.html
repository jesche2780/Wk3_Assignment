Our Story

1982


Our founders started off as a simple farm selling milk products straight from the farm. We were a simple folk.

1995

A manager walked onto the farm one day and talked to the owners about starting a shop on the farm. They agree, the manager starts to create numerous milk products including yogurts and cheeses.

2009

The manager creates our trademark cheese curds. They are hit locally, their delicious flavor spreads throughout the state.

2010

Due to the sad passing of the original manager of the farm, a new manager is found. She incorporates the original shop and opens a new one within the city. The curds continue to be a hit.

2018

The company spreads throughout the state, spreading past the boundaries, and becomes a major distributor of dairy products.
